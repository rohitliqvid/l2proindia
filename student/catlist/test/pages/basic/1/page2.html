Role of Intellectual Property in Growing Your Business
 
 
 

In today’s world driven by innovation, creativity, and technology; IP is an important asset for any business. It can help you and your business in more than one way. There are numerous benefits of IPRs. Some of them are:

  • Stops your competitors from copying your innovations and ideas so that you can gain higher market share or higher profit margin.
  • Investors, especially venture capitalists, want to know that your technology and competitive advantage is protected by patents. So, getting patents can help you attract money from outside investors to grow your business.
  • Patents can improve a company’s reputation and product image.
  • When collaborating with other companies, patents and copyrights can help protect your innovations and ideas that you developed outside of the collaboration .
  • Gives you the right to take legal action against anyone who makes unauthorized use of your intellectual property by stopping the unauthorized use by others and/or by allowing you to seek damages.
  • Allows you to monetize your IP and earn revenue by transacting it through an assignment/license.

There can be significant benefits in collaborating with other companies, especially for start-ups or SMEs, but this comes at the risk of knowledge transfer. Protecting your most important inventions and ideas with patents and copyrights can reduce the risks of this knowledge transfer.
